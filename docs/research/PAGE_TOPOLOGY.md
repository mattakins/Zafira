# Zafira Home Page Topology

1. Header
   - Sticky logo bar, cart icon and count.
   - Interaction model: static/sticky.

2. Hero
   - Mint background, rating line, large headline, support copy, CTA, bottle/saffron image.
   - Interaction model: static with CTA anchor.

3. Press Bar
   - Dark green strip with press names.
   - Interaction model: static.

4. Recovery Foundation Explanation
   - Illustration on left, explanatory copy and bullet proof points on right.
   - Interaction model: static.

5. Triple Depletion Complex
   - Text-heavy protocol explanation plus clinical stat visual.
   - Interaction model: static.

6. Product Purchase Section
   - Product gallery, rating, product copy, benefit bullets, bundle choices, add-to-cart button, counterfeit warning.
   - Interaction model: click-driven bundle selector.

7. Proof + FAQ
   - Customer quote and collapsible FAQ items.
   - Interaction model: FAQ accordions.

8. Ingredients
   - Large ingredient graphic and ingredient cards.
   - Interaction model: static with CTA anchor.

9. Review Band
   - Dark green customer review cards.
   - Interaction model: static carousel approximation.

10. Scientific Background
   - Explanatory copy and image.
   - Interaction model: static with CTA anchor.

11. Customer Wall
   - Multi-card customer quote grid.
   - Interaction model: static.

12. Footer
   - Logo, email-list text, shop links, information links.
   - Interaction model: static.
